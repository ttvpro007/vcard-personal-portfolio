About me

I dream in code and wake up ready to bring those dreams to life!

I've been a gamer at heart for as long as I can remember, ever since my Nintendo Gameboy Color Pokémon Edition sparked my love for games. Now, I bring my childhood dreams to life by transforming complex ideas into real-world applications, from visualizations to simulations and user interfaces for cutting-edge technology.

Though my path initially led me to nursing, where I found joy in caring for others, my passion for game development never faded. So, I took a leap and returned to what truly lights me up—creating games. Now, I work alongside fellow developers, crafting games and tools for Unity, always eager to learn and grow as a developer.

When I'm not coding, I'm likely gathered with my friends for a board game session, strategizing and enjoying some quality competition. Whether in gaming or in life, I'm committed to growing, learning, and sharing my passion with others.
